BIOGRAPHY
My name is Patrick Martinez. I was born in Dallas, Texas. I have lived in Texas all of my life spending a few years in Dallas, Ft. Worth, Laredo and San Antonio before finally settling in Austin. Austin is a booming tech city with not so endearing traffic problems.
