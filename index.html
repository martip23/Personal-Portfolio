BIOGRAPHY
My name is Patrick Martinez. I was born in Dallas, Texas. I've lived a few years in Dallas, Ft. Worth, Laredo and San Antonio before finally settling in Austin.
I am an education and technology expert. I have had success teaching adult basic education such as GED, English as a Second Language and Digital Literacy skills.
I began my studies at Austin Community College where I graduated with an Associates of Science in Computer Science. I am continuing my studies at Texas State University where I seek a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science.
